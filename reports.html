Steps to reconcile Motor Bros data with QuickBooks Online.

✅ QuickBooks Integration Workflow

Since this system runs locally in the browser, sync with QuickBooks via CSV export or Zapier automation.

  1. 1 Go to any report tab and click Export CSV to download the data.
  2. 2 In QuickBooks Online, go to Transactions → Import and upload the CSV.
  3. 3 Map fields: Amount → Amount, Date → Date, Description → Memo.
  4. 4 For vehicle sales, categorize as Sales Revenue; for expenses, use Cost of Goods Sold.
  5. 5 For installment payments, create a Customer Balance (Accounts Receivable) entry in QBO.
